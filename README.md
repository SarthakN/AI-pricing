# AI Pricing Strategy

AI Pricing Strategy is a practical guidance repository for product, business, and engineering teams building or extending products with AI capabilities. It helps organizations define the right pricing strategy and tactical plans for AI features, products, and go-to-market execution.

## 🎯 Purpose

This project aims to help organizations:

- Understand where AI creates product value and customer outcomes
- Select pricing models that align with AI costs, user adoption, and value capture
- Design pricing tiers, feature packaging, and usage metrics for AI-driven solutions
- Plan sales and customer success motions for AI products
- Avoid common pricing pitfalls for AI products (over/under-pricing, commoditization, disjoint value metrics)

## 🔍 What’s included

- Strategic frameworks for AI product pricing
- Tactic-level guidance on packaging, metering, billing, and adoption
- Example pricing decision workflows for AI features in SaaS and enterprise
- Business-case discovery checklists for AI value drivers

## 🚀 Who should use this

- Product managers and PM leaders in AI product teams
- Go-to-market, pricing, and revenue strategy teams
- Founders and startup leaders building AI-enabled products
- Finance and operations teams setting AI usage economics

## 🧭 How to use this repository

1. Start with product value discovery: map AI use cases to customer outcomes.
2. Identify cost levers and budget drivers (model compute, data, engineering, support).
3. Choose pricing patterns: usage-based, seat-based, outcome-based, value-based, or mixed.
4. Define clear pricing tiers with differentiated AI capabilities and service levels.
5. Validate with customer interviews, pilots, and real usage data.
6. Iterate pricing with usage telemetry, segmentation, and win/loss insights.
